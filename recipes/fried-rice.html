Fried Rice


Description

A simple fried rice dish

Ingredients

  • One Serving of Rice
  • Two Eggs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • One Cup of Peas & Carrots
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients - Preferably the Eggs by Whisking Them
  2. Gather a Medium-Sized Pan (Or Preferably a Wok) and Heat it over High-Heat for 30 Seconds
  3. Pour Enough Oil to Cover Half the Pan
  4. Add Peas & Carrots and Fry Until Tender
  5. Add Scrambled Eggs and Stir with the Veggies Until Thickened
  6. Add the Rice and Stir Until Even Spread
  7. Add 2 Tbsp of Soy Sauce and Mix Throughouly
  8. After Mixing the Sauce Throughouly, the Dish is Complete and Enjoy!!!

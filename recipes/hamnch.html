Ham & Cheese

Description


Simple Ham & Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Two Slices of Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Condiments of Choice

Steps

  1. Place Two Pieces of Bread Facing Down
  2. (Skip the Step if no Condiment) Use Choice of Condiment and Spread Enough to Cover One Piece of Bread
  3. Place One Slice of Cheese on Each Piece of Bread
  4. Place the Preferred Amount Ham on Top of the Cheese
  5. Sandwich the Two Pieces of Bread Together with the Ingredients in the Middle
  6. Enjoy!!!

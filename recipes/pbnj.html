Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

Easy Pb&J Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Two Slices of Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Place Two Pieces of Sliced Bread Facing Down
  2. Use Any Spreading Utensil and Use Enough PB to Cover One Entire Piece of Bread
  3. Do the Same for the Other Piece of Bread but with Jelly
  4. Piece the Two Pieces of Bread Together with the Wet Ingredients in the Middle
  5. Enjoy!!!
